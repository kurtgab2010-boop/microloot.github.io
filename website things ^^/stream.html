Stream Flow

The stream will start somewhere around 2PM(GMT +8) on the streaming platform Twitch and will feature our video cameras

the stream will go on for 51 minutes from 2PM.





2:00PM - 2:05PM

History with microtransactions

We will talk about our history with microtransactions as the hosts of the stream,
and we will also ask our audience what their encounters or engagement with microtransactions.

the time alloted will be 5 minutes.




2:05PM - 2:10PM

Context of microtransactions

For the second part of our live stream,
we will give context on microtransactions; where they exist, how they look like, and what they do.
We will also provide a presentation about the topic from there on out.

the time alloted will be 5 minutes.



2:10PM - 2:40PM

Role of microtransactions and presence in games

As to show our audience a visual example of microtransactions, we will provide gameplay of those games that contain microtransactions.
We will also discuss about the roles of microtransaction in videogames, whether they are implemented in mechanisms or a part of progression.

the time alloted will be 30 minutes.



2:40PM - 2:45PM

Types of microtransactions

After the live gameplay footage we have provided, we will discuss a bit on the types of microtransactions we encountered or overall.
The presentation will then be on display again to resume our presentation.

the time alloted will be 5 minutes.



2:45PM - 2:50PM

Potential motives someone has for microtransaction use

After laying out the context on microtransactions and discussing about our history with them, we will then shortly discuss the potential
someone may have to use or purchase microtransactions.

the time alloted will be 5 minutes.



2:50PM - 2:51PM

Key takeaways and Closing remarks

Before concluding the stream, we will give our key takeaways from the live stream, what we learned, and a basic summary.
This is also the time when we stop streaming and only show our video cameras.

the time alloted will be 1 minute.
