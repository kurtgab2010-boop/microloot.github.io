2:00PM - 2:05PM

History with microtransactions

We will talk about our history with microtransactions as the hosts of the stream,
and we will also ask our audience what their encounters or engagement with microtransactions.

The time allotted will be 5 minutes.




2:05PM - 2:10PM

Context of microtransactions

For the second part of our live stream,
we will give context on microtransactions; where they exist, how they look like, and what they do.
We will also provide a presentation about the topic from there on out.

The time allotted will be 5 minutes.




2:10PM - 2:40PM

Role of microtransactions and presence in games

As to show our audience a visual example of microtransactions, we will provide gameplay of some games that contain microtransactions.
We will also discuss the roles microtransactions play in videogames, whether they are implemented as mechanics or as part of progression.

The time allotted will be 30 minutes.




2:40PM - 2:45PM

Types of microtransactions

After the live gameplay footage is shown, we will discuss the types of microtransactions we encountered, or microtransactions in general.
The presentation will then be displayed again to continue our discussion.

The time allotted will be 5 minutes.




2:45PM - 2:50PM

Potential motives someone has for microtransaction use

After laying out the context of microtransactions and sharing our experiences with them, we will discuss the potential reasons
someone may have for purchasing or using microtransactions.

The time allotted will be 5 minutes.




2:50PM - 2:51PM

Key takeaways and closing remarks

Before concluding the stream, we will share our key takeaways, what we learned, and provide a basic summary.
This is also when we end the stream and switch to our video cameras only.

The time allotted will be 1 minutes.
